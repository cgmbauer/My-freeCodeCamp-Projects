The Greatest Band In The World

Tenacious fucking D is an American duo founded in Los Angeles, California, in 1994, by the actors and musicians Jack Black and Kyle Gass. It’s also known as the greatest band in the world.

Introduction To The D.

Tenacious D is well known for its satirical style, playing with the clichés of heavy metal bands. Both their music and their performance on stage are portrayed in a storytelling format, narrating the duo’s sexual prowess and mystical journeys. Jack Black and Kyle Gass make use of pseudonyms, taking on the roles of JB and KG, or even Jables and Kage. During their presentations, the duo is frequently assisted with other musicians, playing other instruments, such as drums, bass and electric guitars. They also work with the D when it comes to record an new album. Nowadays the band is composed by:

  • Jack Black - Vocals / Base Guitar;
  • Kyle Gass - Guitar Solo/ Vocals;
  • John Konesky - Electric Guitar;
  • John Spiker - Bass;
  • Scott Seiver - Drums.
